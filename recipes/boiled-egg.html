How to boil an egg

Boiled eggs are a good source for protein and they are easy to make too

contents

  1. 2 or 3 eggs
  2. water
  3. a pot
  4. fire
  5. salt
  6. bowl

Step by step follow through...

  1. pour reasonable amount of water into the pot
  2. put the eggs gently into the water
  3. heat up the pot which contains egg and water
  4. wait for 15 minutes
  5. turn off the gas or extinguish the fire ( unless you got other things to do with it )
  6. take the eggs and put them in a bowl
  7. pour water into it to make the eggs less hot
  8. peel the eggs
  9. put a bit of salt on the egg and enjoy the proteins
