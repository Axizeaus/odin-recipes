How to make an instant noodle

Instant noodles aren't known for it's health benefits but some people have to rely on it
as their main meal for dinner. I believe they are alright for once in a while though.

contents

  1. a package of instant noodle from your local grocery store
  2. boiling water
  3. an egg
  4. some vegetables that'd go along with noodle
  5. a pot
  6. fire
  7. fork or chop sticks

Step by step follow through...

  1. unpack the instant noodle package
  2. put the square looking curly noodle lookalike into the pot
  3. pour boiling water into it and heat up the pot from below
    • chop the veggies
    • break the egg
    • unpack seasoning packages
  4. put the egg and veggies and seasonings into the pot
  5. stir it up a bit
  6. turn off the gas or extinguish the fire ( unless you got other things to do with it )
  7. eat it using chopstick or fork and enjoy your belly filling up with food
